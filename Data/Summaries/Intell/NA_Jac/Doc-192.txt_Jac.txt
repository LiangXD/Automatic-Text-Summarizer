In 1996-2000, Xinjiang completed a big number of major transportation, energy, water conservancy and telecommunications projects with 254 billion yuan of fixed asset investment.In 2001, the region's investment in fixed assets increased 15 percent year on year to 70.6 billion yuan, with investment in technical renovation and real estate development up 23.9 and 65.5 percent, respectively.Fixed asset investment planned for this year will be 80.7 billion yuan, an increase of 15.2 percent.
