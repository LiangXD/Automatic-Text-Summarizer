The purpose of the trip of Hugo Chavez is to find ways of effectively protecting the oil market and the world oil prices, as well as the interests of Venezuela as one of the world's biggest oil exporters."Relations with Venezuela are important for Russia, and twice as important considering the fact that at present Venezuela is the chairperson at OPEC," President Putin said.This is the second visit of Hugo Chavez to Russia this year.He said in this connection that Russia supported the keeping of oil prices "within the just range."
