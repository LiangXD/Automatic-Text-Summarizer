SHUTTLEWORTH-MBEKI by Mariette le Roux BLOEMFONTEIN April 27 Sapa MBEKI SPEAKS TO 'AFRONAUT' IN OUTER SPACE Contact was made on Saturday between outer space and the Free State city of Bloemfontein when President Thabo Mbeki spoke to Africa's first astronaut Mark Shuttleworth, aboard the International Space Station."I have never seen anything as beautiful as earth from space.Shuttleworth agreed that this was essential towards Africa's rebirth.Shuttleworth described his journey so far as very exciting, saying the flight had been smooth and everything was going according to plan.The president then toasted Shuttleworth with a glass of champagne, saying: "I am sure this will go down in our history as one of those moments where we made quite a leap forward."I am sure the American food will be just as good," Shuttleworth told the president.
