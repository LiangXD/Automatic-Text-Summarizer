Attorney General Francisco Cayuela confirmed that preliminary investigations indicate that the gang of Mexican Saul 'El Guero' Medina specialized in smuggling illegal aliens from the Middle East, in addition to car theft. Medina, who was arrested in the Mexican city of Matamoros off the Gulf of Mexico and which borders Brownsville (Texas, mid-south), is being investigated by the US Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) and Guillermo Narvaez, Tamaulipas Ministerial Police Chief of Police, confirmed. Narvaez explained that the FBI has requested information on this gang, which used false documents such as resident cards, licenses, social security numbers, and seals of the State of Texas Justice Department.
