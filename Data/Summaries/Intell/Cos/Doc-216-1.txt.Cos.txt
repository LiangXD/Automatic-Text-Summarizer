Lawyers for Zumrati Juma, mother of Feroz Abassi, on Wednesday [6 March] added that they would press the British government to take action to shut down the detention camp at Guantanamo Bay, Cuba, where 300 prisoners, including five Britons, are being held. The Red Cross among other international organisations has criticised the United States, saying all prisoners should be protected under international law until a tribunal decides their fate.
