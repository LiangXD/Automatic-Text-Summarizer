Valley Federal Savings & Loan Association took an $89.9 million charge as it reported a third-quarter loss of $70.7 million, or $12.09 a share. The Van Nuys, Calif., thrift had net income of $132,000, or three cents a share, a year ago. The thrift said that after these charges and assuming no dramatic fluctuation in interest rates, the association expects to achieve near record earnings in 1990.
