Last month members of the Umbutfo Swaziland Defence Force [USDF] found 594 illegal aliens in the country. According to the army spokesman, Corporal Nhlanhla Dlamini, the key areas where most of the immigrants were found was Lomahasha, Mhlumeni and Sitsatsaweni. We have discovered that there is a certain family around Lomahasha, which is suspected of assisting illegal immigrants with shelter and transport.
