7 Kyodo -- Defense Agency Director General Gen Nakatani left Friday for the United States, Indonesia and East Timor, government officials said. The defense chief will be in Indonesia on Monday flying in via Hong Kong for talks with his local counterpart and other defense officials. He plans to arrive in East Timor on Wednesday for an inspection and to meet with local leaders and United Nations officials to discuss the possibility of sending members of Japan's Self-Defense Forces to a U.N. peacekeeping mission there.
