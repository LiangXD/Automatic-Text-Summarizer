norway should be in the forefront of combating climate problems. the government wants to point to a significant reduction of climate gate emissions before 2005 after norway has ratified the kyoto protocol in the course of the year, environmental protection minister borge brende has written. regardless of what norway and other countries manage to do in this respect, we must prepare ourselves for climate changes. in recent years the regclim research project, under the aegis of the norwegian research council, has worked to estimate the way the climate will develop in norway in 30 to 50 years. norway and other industrialized countries, on the other hand, have fairly good prospects of grappling with climate changes because we have an economy to put necessary measures to work. there exists the potential for significant reductions in climate gas emissions in norway that will not produced with today's resources. regardless of the shape of the measures in norway, norwegian businesses as well ought to contribute to reducing emissions in poor countries.
