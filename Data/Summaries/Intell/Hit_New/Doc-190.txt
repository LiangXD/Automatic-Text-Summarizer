moscow, october 22 (itar-tass) - russia is actively holding consultations with the oil exporting countries, president vladimir putin said at a meeting with venezuelan president hugo chavez in the kremlin on monday. relations with venezuela are important for russia, and twice as important considering the fact that at present venezuela is the chairperson at opec, president putin said. in his turn, president hugo chavez of venezuela expressed gratitude to putin for the generosity of his soul. he thanked the russian leader for meeting him immediately after his return from the shanghai summit and the visit to dushanbe. hugo chavez is visiting moscow within the framework of a long tour, which includes some european countries, algeria, iran, libya and saudi arabia. the purpose of the trip of hugo chavez is to find ways of effectively protecting the oil market and the world oil prices, as well as the interests of venezuela as one of the world's biggest oil exporters. this is the second visit of hugo chavez to russia this year.
