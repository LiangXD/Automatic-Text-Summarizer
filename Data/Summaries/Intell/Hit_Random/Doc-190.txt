moscow, october 22 (itar-tass) - russia is actively holding consultations with the oil exporting countries, president vladimir putin said at a meeting with venezuelan president hugo chavez in the kremlin on monday. relations with venezuela are important for russia, and twice as important considering the fact that at present venezuela is the chairperson at opec, president putin said. he stressed he was especially satisfied with the fact that the russian-venezuelan dialogue has become stable and dynamic. in his opinion, this is a positive factor, especially from the point of view of ensuring stable oil supply on the world market. late last week the price of crude oil went down at stock exchanges to 19.07 dollars per barrel, which is the lowest level since 1999. the purpose of the trip of hugo chavez is to find ways of effectively protecting the oil market and the world oil prices, as well as the interests of venezuela as one of the world's biggest oil exporters. the first one took place last may, when he held talks with russian leaders.
