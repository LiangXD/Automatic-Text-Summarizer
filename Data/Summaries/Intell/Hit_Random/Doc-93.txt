new york 1997-03-31 coram healthcare corp stock fell monday after the company said its merger partner integrated health services inc lowered the amount it will pay for coram , traders said. in afternoon new york stock exchange trade , coram shares were off 3.12 percent , or 1/8 , to 3-7/8 in above-average trading volume , while integrated shares gained 0.9 percent , or 1/4 , to 29-1/4. integrated health said monday that based on the closing price of its stock on the last business day before execution of the new terms , it would be paying $4.35 per coram share. when the merger was first announced last october , it said coram holders would get about $5.50 worth of integrated stock for each coram share under the previous , fixed ratio. the deal then was worth $655 million , $280 million of that contained in the equity part of the transaction and the balance in the assumption of coram debt and other financial obligations. coram has been given more certainty of the merger closing by virtue of the $25 million breakup fee _ contained in the new terms that go into effect this friday. i still like the deal. _ coram also announced this morning a fourth quarter loss of $0.75 per share , or $31.6 million , including a $15.1 million provision for settlement of a shareholder lawsuit , versus a year-ago loss of $0.45 per share , or $17.6 million.
