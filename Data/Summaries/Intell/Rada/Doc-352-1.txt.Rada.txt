Mudede said on state television that Mugabe was re-elected with 1,685,212 votes against 1,258,758 votes for opposition challenger Morgan Tsvangirai. Mugabe passed the 50 per cent mark with only six districts left to be declared, official figures showed on Wednesday. Results broadcast on state television gave Mugabe 1,634,382 votes, or 52 per cent of the 3.1 million ballots cast. His main rival Morgan Tsvangirai of the Movement for Democratic Change (MDC) had 1,185,890 votes, or 38 per cent of the votes cast. We have a national executive meeting... where we will be looking at the figures and give the reaction of the party, said David Coltart, MP for the Movement for Democratic Change (Mdc).
