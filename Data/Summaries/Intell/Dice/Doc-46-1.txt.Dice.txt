CAMBODIA : Cambodian grenade attack rattles investors. Cambodian grenade attack rattles investors. For many investors , the attack was a reminder that four years into democratic and economic reforms , Cambodia remains a risky place to do business. Political strains have already led to the annulment of three contracts with the country's largest investor , Ariston Sdn Bhd of Malaysia. Diplomats and business representatives said they had received numerous phone calls from worried investors and prospective investors since Sunday's attack.
