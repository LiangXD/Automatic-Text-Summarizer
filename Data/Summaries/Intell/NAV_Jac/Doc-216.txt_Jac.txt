Lawyers for Zumrati Juma, mother of Feroz Abassi, on Wednesday [6 March] added that they would press the British government to take action to shut down the detention camp at Guantanamo Bay, Cuba, where 300 prisoners, including five Britons, are being held."And we are seeking a declaration that the British government demands that Abbasi should not face trial in a military tribunal with the death penalty."
