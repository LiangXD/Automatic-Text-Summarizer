Brazil and Germany call for implementation of Kyoto Protocol by September Brasilia, 14 (Agencia Brasil - ABr) - In a document entitled Partnership Brasil-Germany: Action Plan, both countrires have expressed interest in seeing the Kyoto Protocol take effect by the Sustainable Development Summit (Rio+10), that takes place in South Africa, next September. The document also states that Brazil and Germany intend to expand cooperation in the area of environment and sustainable development.
