Poughkeepsie Savings Bank said a plan to sell its South Carolina branch offices to First Citizens Bank, of Columbia, S.C., fell through. 21. Among other reasons, high fees regulators imposed on certain transfers of thrift deposits to commercial banks substantially altered the economics of the transaction for both parties, Poughkeepsie said. In the year-earlier third quarter, Poughkeepsie Savings had net income of $2.8 million, or 77 cents a share.
