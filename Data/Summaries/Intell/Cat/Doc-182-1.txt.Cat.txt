AYDIN, BODRUM, AMASYA Nov 23 (A.A) - Security forces intercepted on Friday 129 people in Didim township of western province of Aydin and in Bodrum township of western province of Mugla as they were trying to proceed to Greece illegally. A total of 40 Iraqis and Iranians, who entered the country through illegal ways, were captured in the trunk of the lorry.
