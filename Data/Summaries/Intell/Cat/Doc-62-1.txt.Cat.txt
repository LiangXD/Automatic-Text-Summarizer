RUSSIA : Yeltsin pushes Russia closer to union with Belarus. Timothy Heritage MOSCOW 1997-03-31 President Boris Yeltsin gave the green light on Monday to a draft treaty that would push Russia into a union with Belarus , despite liberals' fears that he might be signing away some of his powers to a hardliner.
