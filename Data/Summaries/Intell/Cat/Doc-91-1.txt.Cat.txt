CHICAGO 1997-03-31 Most IMM currency futures eroded again late and finished spotty pit trading weaker. But currencies glossed over a late renewed fall in the Dow Jones average , which retreated again to more than a 100-point loss , and they instead slipped to pit-session lows. Did someone change the rules while I was at lunch ? _ Currencies gave back the bulk of morning gains in late trading that some brokers called sloppy and thin after a morning of sometimes-brisk turnover. Futures had risen early to morning peaks after U.S. stocks resumed Thursday's slide and also tugged Treasury bond futures briefly lower.
