coram eases after integrated deal terms cut. coram eases after integrated deal terms cut. new york 1997-03-31 coram healthcare corp stock fell monday after the company said its merger partner integrated health services inc lowered the amount it will pay for coram , traders said. in afternoon new york stock exchange trade , coram shares were off 3.12 percent , or 1/8 , to 3-7/8 in above-average trading volume , while integrated shares gained 0.9 percent , or 1/4 , to 29-1/4. integrated health said monday that based on the closing price of its stock on the last business day before execution of the new terms , it would be paying $4.35 per coram share. when the merger was first announced last october , it said coram holders would get about $5.50 worth of integrated stock for each coram share under the previous , fixed ratio. coram has been given more certainty of the merger closing by virtue of the $25 million breakup fee _ contained in the new terms that go into effect this friday.
