Google Proposes Android Revenue for Oracle: Filing Google is willing to pay Oracle a percentage of Android revenue on two patents if Oracle succeeds in proving patent infringement at an upcoming trial over the Java programming language, according to a court filing.For future damages, Google proposed paying Oracle 0.5 percent of Android revenue on one patent until it expires this December and 0.015 percent on a second patent until it expires in April 2018.In the Tuesday court filing, Google proposed paying Oracle roughly $2.8 million in damages on two patents, covering the period through 2011.
