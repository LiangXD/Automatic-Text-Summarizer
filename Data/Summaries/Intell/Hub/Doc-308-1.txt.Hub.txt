Endeavour Shuttle To Return On Monday The Endeavour shuttle is expected to land on the US Cape Canaveral launch site, Florida, at 08:55 p. m., Moscow time, the Russian Mission Control reported. The primary objective of the shuttle's mission to the ISS was to deliver the 4th ISS crew and research equipment. According to the Mission Control, the shuttle's crew has perfectly accomplished its mission. The 4th long-term expedition to the ISS is led by Russian cosmonaut Yuri Onufriyenko. This crew will stay on-board the ISS till May 2002.
