MOSCOW, October 22 (Itar-Tass) - Russia is actively holding consultations with the oil exporting countries, President Vladimir Putin said at a meeting with Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez in the Kremlin on Monday. He said in this connection that Russia supported the keeping of oil prices within the just range. He reiterated his friendship and Venezuela's enormous interest in maintaining relations with Russia. The purpose of the trip of Hugo Chavez is to find ways of effectively protecting the oil market and the world oil prices, as well as the interests of Venezuela as one of the world's biggest oil exporters. This is the second visit of Hugo Chavez to Russia this year.
