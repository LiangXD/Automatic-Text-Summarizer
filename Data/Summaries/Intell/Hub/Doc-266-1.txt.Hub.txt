Crane Co. said it holds an 8.9% stake in Milton Roy Corp., an analytical-instruments maker, and may seek control of the company. Crane holds 504,200 Milton Roy shares, including 254,200 bought from Sept. Thermo Electron acquired some 6% of Milton Roy's common stock before throwing in the towel and reducing its stake in early September. Gabelli Group began raising its Milton Roy stake in July, and holds 14.6%, according to a recent SEC filing. In the nine months, Milton Roy earned $6.6 million, or $1.18 a share, on sales of $94.3 million.
