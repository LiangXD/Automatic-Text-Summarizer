Nairobi, April 26 Kyodo -- A Kenyan national working for Japan's leading Asahi Shimbun newspaper was shot dead Thursday night in Nairobi in a botched car robbery, police sources said Friday. Ndegwa joined the Asahi Shimbun's Nairobi Bureau in 1989 as a driver assigned to the bureau chief, and also worked as the assistant to the bureau chief. He was the official driver of Nairobi Bureau Chief Shingo Egi at the time of his death.
