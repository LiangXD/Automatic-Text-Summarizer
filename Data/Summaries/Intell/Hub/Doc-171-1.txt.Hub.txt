Moscow, 27 November: Russia intends to modernize and extend the service life of 11 power units in its nuclear power stations by 2005. In the next stage, 2006-2010, the efficiency of existing nuclear power stations will be stepped up by increasing the utilization of current capacity, extending their service life and increasing capacity by using new-generation power units In 2005, the capacity of all Russian nuclear power stations should total 25.24 GW.
