russia needs space tourists if you want a holiday in space and have the means to pay for it, russia offers you the trip of a lifetime. yuri koptev, the director of the russian aerospace agency, rosaviakosmos, confirmed that russia is looking for space tourists to visit the international space station (iss) in october. there is a free place for the flight in october and we are looking for someone who would like to take it , he declared. at present, citizens from poland, japan and the united states are being tested by rosaviakosmos. russia pioneered space tourism in april, 2001, when the californian millionaire denis tito was taken to the iss for a ten-day space trip. in april, 28-year-old south african millionaire mark shuttleworth will visit the station. when reproducing our materials in whole or in part, reference to pravda.ru should be made.
