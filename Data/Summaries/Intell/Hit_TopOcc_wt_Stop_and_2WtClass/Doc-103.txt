oracle to buy cloud-based software maker cleartrial oracle corp. (orcl) agreed to acquire cleartrial llc, a provider of cloud-based software to biopharmaceutical and medical-device companies, as the technology giant continues its push toward software that runs online. financial terms weren't disclosed. oracle has been on a buying spree of late as it looks to tap the fast growth in sales of web-based software, commonly referred to as cloud computing. the company agreed last month to acquire human-resources software maker taleo corp. (tleo) for $1.9 billion and recently bought the customer-service software maker rightnow technologies inc. for $1.43 billion in cash. oracle last week reported its fiscal third-quarter profit rose 18% and revenue was up 3.1% as new licenses continued to offset weaker hardware revenue.
