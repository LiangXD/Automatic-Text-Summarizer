A group of investors led by Giant Group Ltd. and its chairman, Burt Sugarman, said it filed with federal antitrust regulators for clearance to buy more than 50% of the stock of Rally's Inc., a fast-food company based in Louisville, Ky.Giant Group owned 22% of Rally's shares before the initial public offering.The group currently holds 3,027,330 Rally's shares, or 45.2% of its commmon shares outstanding.
