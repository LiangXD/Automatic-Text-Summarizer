A defence spokesman said today the charred remains of three more persons killed in the Varmul encounter on Friday were recovered from the debris of the burnt houses today.A militant whose identity could not be ascertained was killed in an encounter with the troops at Mutgala (Gool) today.Police are investigating.Two militants were killed in an encounter with the troops/SOG [Special Operations Group] at Dhok Wali (Poonch ) during the preceding night.In an exchange of fire between the militants and troops at Sadnara, Panzalla in Varmul district today, a civilian Fayaz Ahmad Mir was killed.
