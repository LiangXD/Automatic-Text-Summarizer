Sale of U.S. power agencies would be complex $- GAO .WASHINGTON 1997-03-31 Selling off federal hydropower plants and power marketing agencies would be very complex and would likely affect electricity prices in some areas , according_to a Congressional report released Monday .Citing advocates for selling the power marketing agencies , the GAO said the private sector would be able to operate and maintain the assets more efficiently than the government .However , the report did not weigh the costs and benefits of a sale , a hot issue for lawmakers looking to balance the budget and for companies pushing for open power markets .USA : Sale of U.S. power agencies would be complex $- GAO .The three power marketing administrations covered in the report supply about 7 percent of the electricity needs of their utility customers , mostly to meet peak demand , at half the rates charged by other utilities , the report said .The General Accounting Office report surveyed all the interests that the government would need to address if it chose to sell the Southeastern , Southwestern and Western Area Power Marketing Administrations .
