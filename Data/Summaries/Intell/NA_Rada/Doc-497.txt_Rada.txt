Last month members of the Umbutfo Swaziland Defence Force [USDF] found 594 illegal aliens in the country.BETHANY - The number of illegal immigrants in Swaziland seems to be increasing.According to the army spokesman, Corporal Nhlanhla Dlamini, the key areas where most of the immigrants were found was Lomahasha, Mhlumeni and Sitsatsaweni.
