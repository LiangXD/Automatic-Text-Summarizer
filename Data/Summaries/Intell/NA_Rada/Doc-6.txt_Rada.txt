Tatiana Vacova BRATISLAVA 1996-08-20 Measures by the National Bank of Slovakia to ease the country's money supply may not be working , as a mounting liquidity surplus continues to drive prices down on the money market , analysts said on Tuesday ."_ The revalued crown boosted the central bank foreign exchange reserves , and eventually the market liquidity , _" said Dalibor Cernicka , a dealer with Istrobanka .They said the market's burgeoning liquidity , which is seen driving rates down by some two percentage points to about 10 percent on all maturities , points to the ineffectiveness of the NBS's recent moves to take money out_of circulation .
