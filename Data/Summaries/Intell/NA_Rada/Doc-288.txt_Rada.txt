Mugabe confident of victory in historic Zimbabwe vote HARARE-Zimbabwean President Robert Mugabe predicted victory Saturday as he cast his ballots in presidential and local elections at a primary school in Harare's working-class suburb of Highfield.Mugabe had to vote at that particular ward in order to be able to cast ballots both for president and for mayor in Harare, which is holding the two elections simultaneously.Mugabe was clearly expected to turn up at Mhofu School in Highfield, where state television had a truck posted and had been broadcasting live since polls opened early Saturday."They don't want the president of Zimbabwe to remain the president of Zimbabwe," he told reporters outside the school.
