TAIWAN : Formosa says would drop China deal if disapproved. Formosa says would drop China deal if disapproved. Central Bank governor Sheu Yuan-dong told lawmakers that an initial central bank inquiry showed Formosa had not illegally sent money to China from Taiwan , state media reported. In June 1996 , Formosa said it and local Fujian authorities would jointly build a 3,600-megawatt plant , easily the biggest Taiwan investment in China and the first in the power sector , which Taipei had made off limits for Taiwan investors. Formosa sought Taiwan approval but backed out two months later after Taiwan President Lee Teng-hui urged local firms to slow their investments in the arch rival Chinese mainland.
