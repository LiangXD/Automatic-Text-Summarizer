Meanwhile, net at GM's finance arm, General Motors Acceptance Corp., fell 3.1%. That decline was reflected in revenue for the GM Hughes unit, which edged down to $2.58 billion from $2.63 billion. In the nine months, GM Hughes net fell 6.6% to $486.6 million, or $1.48 a share, from $521 million, or $1.58 a share. Revenue rose 3.5% to $8.47 billion from $8.18 billion. At GMAC, net dropped 3.1% to $234.5 million from $241.9 million. Revenue rose 12% to $1.37 billion from $1.22 billion. In the nine months, EDS earned $315.8 million, or $2.62 a share, up 13% from $280.7 million, or $2.30 a share. Revenue rose 14% to $4.03 billion from $3.54 billion.
