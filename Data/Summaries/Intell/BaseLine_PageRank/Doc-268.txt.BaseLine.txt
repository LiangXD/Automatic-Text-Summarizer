Catastrophe losses reduced Aetna's net income by $50 million, including $36 million from Hugo. Last year catastrophe losses totaled $5 million, when net was $235.5 million, or $2.07 a share. The insurer has started processing claims from the Northern California earthquake nearly two weeks ago. In New York Stock Exchange composite trading Friday, Aetna closed at $60, down 50 cents.
