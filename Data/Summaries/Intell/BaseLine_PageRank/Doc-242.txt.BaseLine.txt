Esselte AB, the Stockholm office supplies company, as expected, proposed to acquire the 22% it doesn't own of its U.S. unit, Esselte Business Systems Inc. The price in the proposal is $43.50 for each of the 4.9 million shares the parent doesn't own, or $213.2 million. In New York Stock Exchange composite trading, Esselte closed yesterday at $43.50 a share, up $1.
