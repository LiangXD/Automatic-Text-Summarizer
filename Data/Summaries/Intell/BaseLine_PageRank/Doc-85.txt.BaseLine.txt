Steven Scheer NEW YORK 1997-03-31 With the increasing likelihood the Federal Reserve will hike interest rates again by early July , U.S. Treasury market participants said that the bill sector should benefit from rising coupon yields and a big drop in stock prices. Bills $-- primarily three $- and six-month $-- have outperformed the rest of the yield curve since last Tuesday's quarter-point rate hike by the Fed.
