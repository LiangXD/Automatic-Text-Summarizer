Legislators Request Action from the Brazilian Government Concern has spread to Brazilian legislators. Deputy Jose Genoino (Workers Party -- Sao Paulo) said that the wave of looting threatens Argentine democracy. We don't know where it will go, against whom. In addition to Argentina there are other countries in crisis, such as Colombia and Venezuela. Any step backward for democracy on the continent would be a disaster. Brazil cannot stand by with its hands folded just watching all of this, Genoino affirmed. He noted, Brazil should adopt an open posture with relation to Argentina, which is experiencing a tragedy. I hope that this can alert the Brazilian Government to revise its relationship with the IMF.
