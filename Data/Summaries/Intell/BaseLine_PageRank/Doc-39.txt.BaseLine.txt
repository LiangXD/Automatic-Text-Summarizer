SOUTH KOREA : S.Korea Kim's aides deny taking bribes from Hanbo. SEOUL 1997-03-31 A former minister and three close aides to South Korean President Kim Young-sam on Monday admitted receiving money from the founder of the insolvent Hanbo Group , but denied taking bribes. Three ruling party legislators and close aides to President Kim , Hong In-gil , Hwang Byung-tai and Chung Jae-chull also admitted receiving from gifts in cash from the Hanbo founder. Chung Tae-soo , his chief accountant , an opposition legislator and three top bankers are also on trial.
