The 78-year-old former guerrilla leader, who has governed since independence in 1980, took another swipe at the former colonial power Britain and other western nations, which he said have prejudged the vote. Mugabe has faced widespread criticism and sanctions from the European Union and the United States over alleged rights violations and manipulation of the election process.
