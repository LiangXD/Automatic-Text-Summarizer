MOSCOW, October 22 (Itar-Tass) - Russia is actively holding consultations with the oil exporting countries, President Vladimir Putin said at a meeting with Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez in the Kremlin on Monday. Relations with Venezuela are important for Russia, and twice as important considering the fact that at present Venezuela is the chairperson at OPEC, President Putin said. In his opinion, this is a positive factor, especially from the point of view of ensuring stable oil supply on the world market. In his turn, President Hugo Chavez of Venezuela expressed gratitude to Putin for the generosity of his soul. He thanked the Russian leader for meeting him immediately after his return from the Shanghai summit and the visit to Dushanbe.
