April 11 (Interfax) - A source in Russia's Federal Security Service (FSB) said on Thursday that it could be stated with a large degree of confidence that Jordanian-born Chechen rebel warlord Khattab was killed in a Russian intelligence and combat operation. Reports by our informers also confirm the likelihood of his death, the source said. The source, who had taken part in the operation, did not disclose its dates.
