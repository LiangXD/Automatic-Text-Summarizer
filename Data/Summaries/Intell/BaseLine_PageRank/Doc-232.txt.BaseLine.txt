Reference: CEP20020521000259 Moscow Interfax in English 1336 GMT 21 May 02 Supplies of Russian natural gas to the Romanian market were discussed during a meeting between Gazprom CEO Alexei Miller and Romanian Ambassador to Russia Aurel Konstantin Iliye, the company said in a press release. According the release, the sides also discussed gas storage on Romanian territory and the setting up of a joint venture in this area.
