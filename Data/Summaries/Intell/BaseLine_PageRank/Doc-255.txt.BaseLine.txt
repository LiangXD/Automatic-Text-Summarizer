Norton Co. said net income for the third quarter fell 6% to $20.6 million, or 98 cents a share, from $22 million, or $1.03 a share. In the year-earlier quarter, the tax credit was $3.3 million. Operating profit in the company's abrasives segment rose 16% while operating profit in the engineering materials segment rose 2%. Norton announced earlier this month that it was exploring the possible sale of all or part of Eastman Christensen.
