Yuri Koptev, the director of the Russian Aerospace Agency, Rosaviakosmos, confirmed that Russia is looking for space tourists to visit the International Space Station (ISS) in October. At present, citizens from Poland, Japan and the United States are being tested by Rosaviakosmos. Russia pioneered space tourism in April, 2001, when the Californian millionaire Denis Tito was taken to the ISS for a ten-day space trip.
