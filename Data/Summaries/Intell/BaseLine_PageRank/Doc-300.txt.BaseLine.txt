The results of the elections were to be expected in view of the catastrophic economic situation of the majority of the country's citizens. Argentina has an ever-growing rate of poverty, which defies logic given the enormous land/population ratio, with the richest soil in South America. Once again, the governing class of a South American country has proved itself utterly unable to come to terms with the needs of the population, basically because it has become removed from the reality of the country.
