Crane Co. said it holds an 8.9% stake in Milton Roy Corp., an analytical-instruments maker, and may seek control of the company. In New York Stock Exchange composite trading Friday, Milton Roy shares leaped $2, to $18.375 each, while Crane sank $1.125, to $21.125 a share. John M. McNamara, chief financial officer of Milton Roy, said the company has no comment on Crane's filing. Thermo Electron acquired some 6% of Milton Roy's common stock before throwing in the towel and reducing its stake in early September. Gabelli Group began raising its Milton Roy stake in July, and holds 14.6%, according to a recent SEC filing.
