MOSCOW 1997-03-31 Here is an outline of the controversial draft Union Treaty between Russia and Belarus which was approved in principle by Russian leader Boris Yeltsin on Monday. Yeltsin meets Lukashenko on Wednesday to sign the document , prepared by Belarus authorities and Russian Deputy Prime Minister Valery Serov. The draft has not been published officially , but a copy of the document was obtained by Reuters. EXCEPTION : $-- The pact does not affect rights and obligations of the members states under international treaties signed separately.
