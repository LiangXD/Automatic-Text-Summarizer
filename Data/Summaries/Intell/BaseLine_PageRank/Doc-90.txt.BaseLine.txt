USA : US OPTIONS FOCUS/Indexes slow , more declines seen. US OPTIONS FOCUS/Indexes slow , more declines seen. CHICAGO 1997-03-31 Turnover remained relatively slow in major U.S. stock index options despite the continued slide , traders said Monday. OEX was off about 10 points at 744 and the S&P 500. Although options trading indicated investor complacency , some indicators suggested bearishness was on the rise , which is bullish for contrarians.
