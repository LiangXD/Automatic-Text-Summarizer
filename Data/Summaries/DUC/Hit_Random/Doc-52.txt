so far this year, 726 tornadoes have touched down nationwide, well ahead of the 640 tornadoes recorded during the first six months of last year, and the 30-year average of 482 tornadoes recorded between january and july, said frederick ostby of the national severe storms forecast center here. ostby said 101 tornadoes touched down from kansas to kentucky, leaving nine people dead. the study of tornadoes since early this century shows that they are concentrated in regions for five- or six-year periods, said michael smith, president of weatherdata inc. tornadoes have caused 20 deaths this year, while an average year in the 1980s saw 52 deaths, and in the 1970s nearly 100, ostby said. flooding in texas has been blamed for at least 16 deaths since april. an estimated 10,000 people were forced from their homes, and high water caused hundreds of millions of dollars in damage. an estimated 10,000 people were forced from their homes, and high water caused hundreds of millions of dollars in damage.
