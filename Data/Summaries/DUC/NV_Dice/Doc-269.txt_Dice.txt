Lima, 13 Feb (AFP) -- The "Sunday Review" television program said that according to a "very reliable source," the end of the "popular war," the surrender of weapons, and a general amnesty, would be the main points of a peace agreement "without victors or vanquished," between the Shining Path and the Peruvian Government.So far, government authorities have not denied or rejected the accuracy of this agreement between the government and Shining Path.
