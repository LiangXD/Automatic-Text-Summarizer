west german police said one pilot was killed in the in-flight crash. west german police spokesman hugo lenxweiler told the ap in a telephone interview that one of the pilots was killed in the accident. "the other pilot was able to eject safely," lenxweiler said. lenxweiler said he did not know if the pilot who ejected suffered any injuries. identities of the pilots were not immediately released. al sattler said the pilot in the black forest crash ejected safely before the crash and was taken to ramstein air base to be examined. the aircraft was taking part in a nato military tactical air exercise being conducted from the canadian air force base in baden-soellingen, sattler said.
