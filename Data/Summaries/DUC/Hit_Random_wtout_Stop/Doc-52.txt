so far this year, 726 tornadoes have touched down nationwide, well ahead of the 640 tornadoes recorded during the first six months of last year, and the 30-year average of 482 tornadoes recorded between january and july, said frederick ostby of the national severe storms forecast center here. ostby said 101 tornadoes touched down from kansas to kentucky, leaving nine people dead. this moves the boundary of the jet stream to the north, ostby said. tornadoes have caused 20 deaths this year, while an average year in the 1980s saw 52 deaths, and in the 1970s nearly 100, ostby said. "there are better watches, better warnings and a better response," ostby said.
