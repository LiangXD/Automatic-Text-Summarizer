Researchers who developed and tested the medicine report that it "may well be the drug of choice in the treatment of hairy cell leukemia." In all, the Scripps doctors have treated 24 patients with almost complete success. Two other treatments for hairy cell leukemia have been developed in recent years. However, interferon is less effective, and both of those treatments produce serious side effects. Also unlike other treatments, this one appears to work after a single seven-day course of therapy.
