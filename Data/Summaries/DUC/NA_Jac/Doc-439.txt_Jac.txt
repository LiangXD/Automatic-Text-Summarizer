Despite a bumper harvest last year, Malawi has not recovered from the massive 1991/92 drought.Another 270,000 tons would be required to be sold in the market to stabilise food supply and prices.This year's cereal production among smallholders is estimated to be 60 per cent less than last year's bumper harvest, and only 28 per cent higher than in 1991/92, when drought devasted southern Africa.
