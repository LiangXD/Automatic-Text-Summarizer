IRNA reported Saturday that an earthquake registering 5.5 on the Richter scale shook the region Saturday.An observatory in Hong Kong said the quake was centered in the Caspian Sea about 120 miles northeast of the Azerbaijani capital and port city of Baku and measured 6.5 on the Richter scale.Iran is shaken by hundreds of earthquakes a year, ranging from 2.4 to 5.5 on the Richter Scale.
