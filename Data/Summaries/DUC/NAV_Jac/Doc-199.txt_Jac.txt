(Hamburg DPA in Spanish 1636 GMT 24 Mar 94) Lorenzo Menendez, an expert in Mexican affairs at the Cuban Foreign Ministry, today told PRENSA LATINA that Colosio's assassination caused shock on the island.Colosio's assassination was widely covered by Venezuelan news media today.Liberal Party candidate Ernesto Samper, who met with Colosio a few weeks ago, regretted the assassination and said that such violence is the result of "savage capitalism."
